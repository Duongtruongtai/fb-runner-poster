Privacy Policy — Bien Dong Crypto Poster

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This application ("RunnerComVN" app, ID 1382243627187098) is a personal content-publishing tool used solely by its owner to write and schedule posts on Facebook Pages that the owner manages.

1. Data we access

The app accesses only the Facebook Pages managed by its owner, using the permissions pages_show_list, pages_read_engagement, pages_read_user_content, pages_manage_posts, and pages_manage_engagement. It does not access data of any other Facebook user.

2. Data we collect and store

The app does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data of visitors or Page followers. Access tokens are stored locally on the owner's private computer and are never published or transmitted to third parties.

3. How data is used

Data is used exclusively to publish and schedule posts, and to manage comments on the owner's own Pages.

4. Data deletion

To request deletion of any data related to this app, contact the owner at taihocclaudecode@gmail.com. You can also remove the app from your Facebook account at any time via Settings → Apps and Websites, which revokes all its access.

5. Third parties

The app communicates only with the official Facebook Graph API. No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

Bien Dong Crypto Poster — a personal, non-commercial tool.
